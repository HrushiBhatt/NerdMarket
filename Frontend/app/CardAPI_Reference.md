# GET

> api/cards
> Gets all cards. (Not something we should be using too often as it's slow.

> api/cards/{id}
> Gets a specific card by its id. 
 

> api/cards/type/{cardType}
> Gets cards by a card type (mtg, Yu-gi-oh, pokemon)

# Post

> api/cards
> Makes a new card.

# PUT
> api/cards/{id}
> Updates a card by ID.

# DELETE
> api/cards/{id}
> What it says on the tin.
